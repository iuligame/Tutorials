We are Miniclip

An international digital games and entertaiment company.

A leader in multiplayer games, with a purpuse to 'unleash the gamer in everyone' with more than 10 bilion downloads to date.

What we do

We develop, publish and distribute multiplayer mobile games. We reach over 400 milion players each month, and our audience keeps growing.

Our experience, passion, and people provide us with a unique understanding of the digital games space, with extensive worldwide distribution and an internatinally recognised brand.
